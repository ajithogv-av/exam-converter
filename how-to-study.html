How to Study Effectively for Exams

Studying effectively means using time wisely, revising regularly and practicing questions.

1. Use Short Notes

Create summary notes with formulas, facts and key concepts.

2. Practice Questions

Apply what you studied through questions.

3. Revise Weekly

Revision improves long-term memory.

4. Avoid Distractions

Study in focused blocks of 30 to 45 minutes.

5. Use Smart Tools

Use tools that convert long notes into quick revision material.
