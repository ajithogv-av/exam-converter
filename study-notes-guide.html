How to Make Effective Study Notes

Good study notes help students revise faster and remember key concepts easily.

1. Keep Notes Short

Write only formulas, facts, and important concepts.

2. Use Headings

Divide topics into clear sections for quick review.

3. Use Bullet Points

Bullets improve readability and memory.

4. Highlight Important Areas

Mark formulas, dates, definitions and exceptions.

5. Revise Weekly

Update and revise notes regularly.
