How to Prepare for Competitive Exams

Competitive exams require planning, revision, and regular practice. A smart strategy improves success chances.

1. Understand the Syllabus

Know the exam pattern, subjects, and marks distribution.

2. Build a Study Schedule

Set weekly targets and follow a realistic timetable.

3. Practice Mock Tests

Mock tests improve speed, accuracy, and confidence.

4. Revise Regularly

Revision is important for formulas, facts, and concepts.

5. Stay Consistent

Daily progress is more powerful than occasional long study sessions.
