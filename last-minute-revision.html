Last Minute Revision Tips

Use your remaining time smartly and focus on high-value revision.

1. Revise Notes Only

Avoid starting brand-new topics now.

2. Practice Key Questions

Use previous questions and important areas.

3. Flash Revision

Spend 10–15 minutes per topic.

4. Stay Calm

Confidence improves recall.

5. Sleep Well

Rest helps memory more than panic study.
