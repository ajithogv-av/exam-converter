Last Minute Revision Tips

Last minute revision should focus on strengthening what you already know instead of learning new topics.

1. Revise Notes Only

Use your short notes, formulas and summaries.

2. Solve Important Questions

Practice common and previous exam questions.

3. Focus on Weak Areas

Spend extra time on topics you often forget.

4. Stay Calm

A relaxed mind remembers better than a stressed mind.

5. Sleep Properly

Rest before the exam improves concentration.

Final Tip: Smart revision beats panic studying.
