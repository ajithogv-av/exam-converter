Last Minute Revision Tips for Exams

Last minute revision should focus on important topics, formulas, and previously studied notes.

1. Revise Notes Only

Do not start new chapters before exams.

2. Solve Previous Questions

Practice common questions to gain confidence.

3. Use Flash Revision

Spend 15 minutes per topic revising key points.

4. Stay Calm

Sleep properly and avoid panic studying.

5. Use Smart Summaries

Use tools that convert long notes into short summaries.
