Time Management Tips for Exams

Good time management helps students complete syllabus faster and reduce stress before exams.

1. Create a Daily Plan

Assign fixed hours for study, revision and practice tests.

2. Use Priority Order

Study difficult subjects when your energy is highest.

3. Avoid Multitasking

Focus on one topic at a time for better results.

4. Use Study Blocks

Study for 30 to 45 minutes, then take a short break.

5. Track Progress

Check completed chapters weekly and adjust plans.
